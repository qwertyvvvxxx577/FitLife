Privacy policy

Collecting information

We do not collect personal data of our website users. You can freely browse all the materials available on our resource without registering, logging in to your account or providing any personal information.

We respect your right to privacy. No personally identifiable information is stored on our server or databases.

In some cases, we may automatically receive general technical information, such as browser type, operating system version, or time of visit. This information is not used to identify users and is only collected for statistical and technical purposes.

Cookies and advertising

Our website may use cookies, which are small text files that are stored on your browser to improve your experience on the website. Cookies help us understand how users interact with content and enable us to display more relevant information.

We may serve ads through Google AdSense or similar advertising platforms. Google and its partners may use cookies to personalize ads based on your interests and browsing of other sites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by changing your settings in your Google account or on the Your Online Choices.

Read more: How Google uses data.

External links

Our website may contain hyperlinks to other web resources that we believe to be useful or relevant. However, we have no control over these third-party sites and are not responsible for their content, security or privacy practices.

Before submitting any personal information to an external website, we recommend that you read its own privacy policy.

Protection of minors

Our website is aimed at a general audience, including teenagers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe that your child or other minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take the necessary steps to delete such data.

Security

We make every effort to ensure the safety of your stay on the site. Although we do not collect any confidential data, we strive to minimize the risks associated with browsing the resource, in particular by using a secure connection (HTTPS), restricting unauthorized access and checking the scripts used.

Changes to the privacy policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy from time to time. All changes are effective when they are posted on this page. We recommend that you review this page from time to time to be aware of any changes.

Contact us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or would like to learn more about how we handle your data, please email us at yourmail@gmail.com.

We are open to feedback and do our best to make your stay on the site safe and enjoyable.
